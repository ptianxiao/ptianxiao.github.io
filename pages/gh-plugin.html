Grasshopper Plugin

Our Grasshopper plugin integrates seamlessly with Rhino to enhance parametric design capabilities.

Key Features:

  • Custom components for Amphi-Bloc integration
  • AI-assisted generative design tools
  • Optimization algorithms for structural and environmental performance
  • Direct interface with our Voxel and Diffusion Agents

This plugin bridges the gap between our cutting-edge AI technologies and the familiar Grasshopper environment, empowering designers to push the boundaries of parametric architecture.
