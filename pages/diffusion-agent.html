Diffusion Agent

Our Diffusion Agent harnesses the power of diffusion models to generate and refine architectural designs.

Features:

  • AI-powered design generation based on textual descriptions
  • Style transfer and adaptation of existing designs
  • Rapid prototyping and iteration of architectural concepts
  • Integration with other design tools for seamless workflow

The Diffusion Agent is revolutionizing the conceptual phase of architecture, allowing for unprecedented creativity and efficiency.
